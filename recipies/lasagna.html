Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of onion
  • 1/2 cup of green pepper
  • 1/2 cup of garlic
  • 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of salt
  • 1/2 cup of pepper
  • 1/2 cup of oregano
  • 1/2 cup of basil
  • 1/2 cup of lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Brown the ground beef
  2. Add the onion
  3. Add the green pepper
  4. Add the garlic
  5. Add the tomato sauce
  6. Add the tomato paste
  7. Add the salt
  8. Add the pepper
  9. Add the oregano
  10. Add the basil
  11. Add the lasagna noodles
  12. Add the ricotta cheese
  13. Add the mozzarella cheese
  14. Add the parmesan cheese
  15. Bake for 30 minutes
