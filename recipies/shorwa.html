Shorwa

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of lamb
  • 1/2 cup of onion
  • 1/2 cup of garlic
  • 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of salt
  • 1/2 cup of pepper
  • 1/2 cup of turmeric
  • 1/2 cup of cumin
  • 1/2 cup of coriander
  • 1/2 cup of water

Instructions:

  1. Brown the lamb
  2. Add the onion
  3. Add the garlic
  4. Add the tomato sauce
  5. Add the salt
  6. Add the pepper
  7. Add the turmeric
  8. Add the cumin
  9. Add the coriander
  10. Add the water
  11. Simmer for 1 hour
