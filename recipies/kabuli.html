Kabuli Palaw

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of raisins
  • 1/2 cup of carrots
  • 1/2 cup of almonds
  • 1/2 cup of pistachios
  • 1/2 cup of cardamom
  • 1/2 cup of cumin
  • 1/2 cup of salt

Instructions:

  1. Boil the rice
  2. Heat the oil
  3. Add the sugar
  4. Add the raisins
  5. Add the carrots
  6. Add the almonds
  7. Add the pistachios
  8. Add the cardamom
  9. Add the cumin
  10. Add the salt
  11. Mix the rice with the other ingredients
